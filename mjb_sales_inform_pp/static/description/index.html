Message Teams In Sale Order

Under Sales Configuration menu, there you can see new menu Message Teams.

You can add your PMC/Packing team or Logistics team in this.
Note :Enable Active and PMC/Packing Team or Logistics Team Accordingly.

In Sales Order form, you can see two new buttons for sending log note for the PMC/Packing team or Logistics team respectively.

When clicking it will create new logs according to the button and the sale order.

User will get a notification in odoo and a email.
